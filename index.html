About Mindful Haven

Founded by Elsy Nabea, Mindful Haven blends spiritual clarity with practical tools. We create healing programs, books, and community projects to help people recover dignity, purpose, and momentum.

  • • The Healing Room — short, soulful episodes
  • • Beyond the Layoff — the book & toolkit
  • • Bloom Tracker — simple period app for girls
  • • Youth mentorship — Majengo initiative

Mission

Restore hearts, renew minds, and rebuild lives with wisdom-driven tools and community support.

Vision

A generation unchained—emotionally whole, spiritually anchored, and economically empowered.
